Tomato Basil Soup

Tastes like a bowl of sunshine and love!

Everyone needs a feel good dish, and for me, this is it. This dish tastes delicious and warms you up from the inside out! I first tried this is the dead of winter and it literally felt like the sun was in my stomach, in a good way!

Have this as an appetizer to warm up for the main dish, or make a grilled cheese with some vegetarian sausage on the sandwhich for a tasty, well-rounded dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ripe plum tomatoes, cut in half lengthwise
  • 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons good olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped yellow onions (2 onions)
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 (28-ounce) canned plum tomatoes, with their juice
  • 4 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 quart vegetable stock or water
