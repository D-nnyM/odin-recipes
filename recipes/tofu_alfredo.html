Tofu Alfredo

Fettuccine noodles with a creamy, delicious, guilt-free sauce!

Great for a night where no one feels like cooking, this meal super fast and easy, without sacrificing taste! The sauce is healthier for you than all the dairy in regular alfredo, and it's packed with loads of protein, so it's more well balanced as well!

Feel free to add more veggies whichever way you prefer. I usually either sauté or bake mine, depending on the flavor and speed I'm going for.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lite silken tofu
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 teaspoons basil or Italian herb seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 4 tablespoons of vegan margarine
  • 16 oz fettuccine pasta

Directions

  1. Boil water and cook the pasta until al dente
  2. Blend all the other ingredients in a blender until smooth
  3. Sauté or bake any veggies you want to add into the pasta
  4. Pour the sauce over the noodles, add in the veggies, mix well and serve!
